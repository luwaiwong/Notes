---
# Definition
The **Variance** of a [Discrete Random Variable](Discrete%20Random%20Variable.md) *X* is the number. $$Var(X)=E[(X-E[X])^{2}]$$ or alternatively,$$Var(X)=E[X^2]-(E[X])^2$$
$E[X]$ is the [Expected Value](Expected%20Value.md) of X
