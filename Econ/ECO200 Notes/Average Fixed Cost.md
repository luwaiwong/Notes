**Average Fixed Cost** is [Fixed Costs](Fixed%20Cost.md) divided by a firm's **Quantity Produced**

---
# Definition
$$AFC = \frac{FC}{Q}$$
# Properties
**Average Fixed Costs** *decrease* as quantity increases
